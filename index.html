About Me

I am a Generative AI Engineer and researcher with a strong background in deep learning, medical imaging, and intelligent systems. My work focuses on designing AI-driven solutions that bridge theoretical research and real-world applications, particularly in healthcare and automation.

I have contributed to peer-reviewed research in areas such as convolutional neural networks, recurrent architectures, capsule networks, and multimodal learning. My research interests include generative models, medical image segmentation, AI-assisted clinical workflows, and scalable machine learning systems.

Currently, I am developing AI-powered platforms that integrate large language models with operational systems to enhance efficiency, decision-making, and user experience in academic and industry settings.
